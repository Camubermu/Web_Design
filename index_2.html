¿What is Pop it?

"Pop it" is a game for children, whose process can be compared to popping bubble wrap.

But unlike movies, pop-its can be pressed "infinitely" with characteristic

sensations and sounds: simply squeeze dozens of

silicone bubbles in the opposite direction.

Rules of the game

Players take turns and press the bubbles. It is imperative to finish

the row you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same

time (they must be nearby, you can’t press several bubbles at

once if they are not right next to each other). The task is to make sure that

the opponent is the last one to leave. Whoever went last lost!
